Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026  ·  Applies to: Resamaze (iOS)

Overview

Baker Applications ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Resamaze iOS application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

By creating an account or using Resamaze, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our app.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Your email address and password when you create an account. We use Supabase to manage authentication.
  • Resume content: All professional information you enter into the app, including your name, contact details, work experience, education history, skills, projects, and certifications.
  • Application tracking data: Job applications you log in the app, including company names, roles, and status updates.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Feature usage counts (such as the number of AI-powered resume imports you have performed) stored to manage free and paid tier limits.
  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription, managed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store.
  • Device and system information: Basic device type and operating system version, collected by Apple and third-party services listed below. We do not independently collect device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide the core features of Resamaze (resume building, previewing, exporting)
  • To process AI-powered features (resume import, job description scanning) through secure server-side processing
  • To enforce free tier limits and manage your Pro subscription
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset) via Resend
  • To respond to support requests and inquiries
  • To improve the app based on general usage patterns

We do not use your resume content or personal information for advertising purposes, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

AI-Powered Features

Resamaze includes optional AI-powered features (resume import and job description scanning). When you use these features, portions of your resume content or job description text are transmitted to our secure server-side infrastructure for processing. This processing is handled through Anthropic's Claude API.

Data sent for AI processing is used only to generate results for you in the moment. It is not stored by us beyond the processing request and is subject to Anthropic's data usage policies. You can review Anthropic's privacy practices at anthropic.com/privacy.

AI features are optional. The core resume-building functionality does not require transmitting your data off-device.

Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely by Supabase in accordance with industry-standard security practices. Subscription data is stored by RevenueCat. Resume content you enter in the app is held in app memory during your session.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and associated data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within a reasonable time frame, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

To request account deletion, contact us at resamazereset@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Resamaze is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information (you can update most information directly in the app).
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at resamazereset@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
