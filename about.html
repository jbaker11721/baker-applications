About

Building tools for modern professionals.

Baker Applications is an independent iOS app studio. We build focused productivity tools that help professionals organize, present, and advance their careers.

What we do

We design and develop mobile applications for iOS. Our work centers on professional productivity — tools that are polished, purposeful, and respect your time and attention.

Our flagship app, Resamaze, is a modular resume builder that rethinks how professionals create and manage their resumes. Instead of starting from scratch for every application, Resamaze lets you build a library of reusable content blocks and assemble tailored resumes for each opportunity.

How we operate

We're a small, independent studio. That means every design decision, every line of code, and every support interaction comes from people who genuinely care about the product.

We sustain our work through honest, straightforward pricing — no dark patterns, no surprise charges, no selling your data. When you pay for a Baker Applications product, you're funding continued development and support, nothing else.

Our commitments

Privacy: We collect only what's necessary to run our apps. We never sell personal information or share it for advertising purposes. You can read our full Privacy Policy for specifics.

Quality: We don't ship half-finished products. Features arrive when they're right, and we stand behind the quality of what we release.

Longevity: We build apps we intend to maintain and improve for years. If that changes, we'll communicate clearly and give users time to export their data.
